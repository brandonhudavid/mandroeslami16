Ongoing Research
  The current ongoing research is funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) under grant number ____ and under PI Professor Scott J. Brandenberg, where Soil Structure Interaction of fine grain soils, such as silt and clay mixtures at low plasticity indices under cyclic and monotic loading, has been the main goal.
  For this purpose diffferent clay-silt mixtures were prepered to have similar PI's, having either fresh water or saline water within the mixture as the pore fluid. This was checked through running Atterberg limits tests to arrive at the target PI of about 10%.
  Consolidation tests have been conducted on these two different mixtures of saline water and fresh water, and differences in consolidation behavior of these soil mixtures have been observed.
